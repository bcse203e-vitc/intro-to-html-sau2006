Definition List

Platelets
Platelets are an essential component of blood used in blood clotting.
WBCs
WBCs/White Blood Cells are of various types and aid in protecting our body against diseases.
RBCs
RBCs/Red Blood Cells are the main component of blood, giving it its rich crimson colour due to the presence of haemoglobin.
Plasma
The medium in which all the cells are dispersed.
Erythrocytes
Another name for RBCs.
